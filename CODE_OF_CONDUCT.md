# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The CPSC community is committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for everyone interested in constraint-projected computing research and development.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Focus on technical merit and clear communication
- Acknowledge different perspectives and experience levels
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct inappropriate for a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all CPSC organization spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories, issues, and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Any official CPSC communication channels

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at BitConcepts, LLC. All complaints will be reviewed and may result in:

- Warning
- Temporary or permanent ban from the community
- Removal of contributions
